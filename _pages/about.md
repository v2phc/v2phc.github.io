## What can you find here?

Even though a website is commonly public, I think that in general a blog is also something personal.
And so I've created this not with the aim to share information, but rather as a way for me to write about things that I care about, that I would like to learn, understand, or think about, and also a bit to store this information somewhere (and where better than in "the cloud").
In particular, I hope that through writing I can force - or motivate - myself a bit to more seriously think about a certain topic or questions, and that I can also revisit some of the things that I have thought (or will think) about.
And so while it is too early to give a proper classification (or characterization) of the set of topics that might be discussed, I expect (and hope) that I will write about topics that interest me.
Thus, I will write about things that I know a bit about - or at least, I hope/pretend I do - but also things that I don't anything about but for which I'm just curious.

So I'll write about "cool things" that I've read about in a book, another blog, or learned by watching a youtube video.
Yet, I'll also write about more serious things, such as beliefs, different viewpoints, questions that I find confusing or do not know the answer to, and so forth.
Of course, this does not take away that I might not write some nonsensical things such as some crazy theory or thought experiments.
I'll also write about books - both fiction and non-fiction - such as give a summary, reflect on what I've learned, or share some sentences or paragraphs (i.e., quotes) that I find insightful.

I hope the above already gives you a good idea what to expect, but to be even more concrete I will throw around some ambiguous keywords. 
You might find posts related to religion, informatica (a.k.a., computer science), physics, and last but not least, mathematics.
And so with the previous to previous paragraph in mind, you might some which are more on the philosophical or scientific side.

Of course, this website might be considered incomplete if I do not give explicit description of myself.
This will be discussed in the next (sub)section.
